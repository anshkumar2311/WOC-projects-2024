How It Works?

Apply

Submit your application through our online form. We're looking for passionate African innovators who are eager to make an impact. Fill out your details, tell us about your skills, and share why you want to join OnHack Africa.

Get Selected

Our selection committee will review applications and select the most promising participants. If you're chosen, you'll receive an email with all the details you need to prepare for the hackathon.

Build in Real Life (IRL)

Join us for the hackathon, where you'll meet your fellow participants, form teams, and start building. For three weeks, you'll be immersed in a high-energy environment, working on innovative projects, attending mentor sessions, and enjoying fun activities like game nights.
Weekly
Schedule Breakdown

Week1

Ideation and Team Formation

Day 1-2: Kickoff event, team formation, and brainstorming sessions.

Day 3-5: Idea validation, project planning, and initial prototyping.

Day 6-7: Mentor feedback sessions and refining project concepts.

Week2

Building and Development

Day 1-5: Heads down building, coding, and daily progress sharing.

Wednesday: Game Night! Smashkart competition with prizes.

Friday: Game Night! Another chance to compete and unwind.

Week3

Testing and Final Presentation

Day 1-3: Finalizing projects, user testing, and bug fixing.

Day 4-5: Preparing for the final presentation, pitch practice.

Day 6: Demo Day! Present your project to judges and potential investors.

Day 7: Closing ceremony, awards, and celebration.

Special Activities

Wednesday & Friday

Game Night featuring intense Smashkart competitions. The winner gets a tip from the OnHack.
